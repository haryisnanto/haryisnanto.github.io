Project information

  • Name: Sedana (Savings & Loans Management System)
  • Client: BUMDES Singaraja

About Project

Sedana is a savings and loans information system owned by Dimata IT Solution. Designed to helps micro-credit business to record savings and loans transactions for their customers and provides the information they need for further action. Not only for record data, it also has features that help users to simplify the process by automatically generate data they need such as payment schedule and interest value. This system is made by following the common standard rules for savings and loans business, but still can be customize based on client's need.
